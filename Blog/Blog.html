The Future of Web Development

December 15, 2025

Web development continues to evolve at a rapid pace, with new frameworks and technologies emerging regularly. As we look toward the future, several trends are shaping how we build and interact with web applications. From serverless architectures to progressive web apps, developers are finding innovative ways to create faster, more efficient, and user-friendly experiences.

One of the most significant shifts we're seeing is the move toward component-based development and the adoption of modern JavaScript frameworks. These tools not only improve developer productivity but also enhance the overall user experience by enabling more dynamic and responsive interfaces. As browsers become more capable and internet speeds increase globally, we can expect even more sophisticated web applications that blur the line between web and native apps.

Using a Big Goal for Leverage

December 15, 2024

Motivation for me wasn’t a magical thing, but it was surprisingly effective, so I might as well say it was. I created a Big Goal — an achievement I hoped for in no more than a year or two that would make a real difference for me. My goal was not to live life on the same financial level that I had in Knoxville, but to move to Seattle, where costs are roughly twice as high. A reasonable thing to aspire to, but it won’t be easy. I figured learning web development would help me achieve my goals.

Why I wanted to move isn’t important, but it kept me thinking about it constantly. Since taking on every part of a career as a freelancer would be hard work (learning, looking for jobs, setting my prices, doing the paperwork, considering offers…), I came up with several ways to remember the goal. Looking for fantasy apartments was something I often did on Craigslist. To remind myself of what I’m aiming for, I put posters in my work area. I signed up for emails about what’s going on in Seattle. When the going got rough, I tried to remember what my goal was as much as I could.
